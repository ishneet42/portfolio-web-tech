Projects

👉🏻SheWell

Overview: SheWell is a web application developed collaboratively during the HackWie-Hackathon, with the aim of addressing the wellness needs of individuals dealing with Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOD). As part of a dynamic team of four members, we set out to create a platform that offers resources, tools, and support for managing PCOD-related concerns and promoting overall well-being.

👉🏻Crop Recommendation System

The Crop Recommendation System is a web application designed to assist farmers in selecting suitable crops for cultivation based on various environmental and soil parameters. Leveraging Machine Learning, the Django framework and a combination of frontend and backend technologies, the system streamlines the crop selection process, ultimately optimizing agricultural productivity and resource utilization.

👉🏻ZenithCare

Zenith Care has made significant strides in providing accessible and holistic healthcare services to its users, with a daily traffic of around 30 users per day. By offering a user-friendly platform with essential features such as a Contact Us form, the website has effectively facilitated communication between healthcare providers and patients, ultimately enhancing the quality and accessibility of healthcare services.

Visit www.zenithcare.in for more details.

👉🏻CrimsonConnect

blockchain-based blood donation platform developed for xxnetwork. Leveraging the robust capabilities of the Django framework, we crafted a solution that not only addresses critical issues in the blood donation process but also champions decentralization and user privacy. By incorporating Polygon, xxnetwork, and Ethereum AB blockchain technologies, the platform ensures seamless transactions and heightened security, crucial elements in handling sensitive medical data and facilitating blood donations. It has a reward system, ingeniously implemented through an NFT marketplace. This not only incentivizes blood donation but also fosters a sense of community engagement and recognition for donors.
